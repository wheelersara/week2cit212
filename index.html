Why Are Glaciers melting?

Glaciers are melting for a multitude of reasons. The result of human activity such as an influx of carbon dioxide and greenhouse gasses being released is a factor. This in turn, makes temperatures higher, and the glaciers melt quicker. The melting of the glacers has effect on the sea levels and has a great number of consequences.
